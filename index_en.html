About 2080 Dental Centre

2080's name originates from the "Oral Health Movement 8020".
Aiming to protect each functional tooth for seniors, ensuring they retain at least 20 functional teeth by the age of 80, helping them maintain the joy of chewing and enjoy the nutrition that food provides.
We are committed to providing comprehensive quality dental care services for all age groups in the community,
from teeth cleaning and fillings to root canal treatments and more, promoting preventive care and jointly practicing a healthy life from the teeth upwards.
We believe that a healthy, complete set of teeth is the foundation of a happy and dignified life in old age.

Services

Oral Examination

Provides comprehensive oral examinations with X-ray services to understand your oral condition for further diagnosis.

Teeth Cleaning and Scaling

Provides professional teeth cleaning and scaling services to help you maintain clean teeth and prevent periodontal disease.

Dental Restoration and Filling

Provides professional dental restoration and filling services, using amalgam, composite resin and glass ionomer cement to help you restore dental function and maintain aesthetics.

Oral Surgery

Provides professional oral surgery services to address complex dental issues. For example, tooth extraction, wisdom tooth removal, and root canal treatment.

Pedodontics

Provides gentle and professional oral care for children.

Dentures and Braces Fabrication

Provides professional dentures and braces fabrication services to help you restore chewing function and maintain aesthetics.
